Target Audience

The target audience is people that are looking for the weather and its conditions, but with a more deep purpose, and that is what makes this page special. They are people that want to travel and get to know the world, and experiment with different feelings and climates. The target audience is more focused on young adults and adults that have the possibility to travel, maybe for business or for fun. Also people that are curiouse, and want to learn about different countries and weathers. The page offers the posibility to share the experience of living in your own country, and help others in their trips, so people with this desire will find this page extremely useful.
