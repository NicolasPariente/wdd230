Can Weather Affect Your Mood?

Rain, rain go away. No, seriously, go away. We're over it. While a change in weather can be a really nice change of pace at times — or even greatly needed in some cases, such as rain during a drought — it can also be...frustrating. When less-than-pleasant weather drags on (and on and on), it can complicate outdoor plans, make traffic miserable and, honestly, feel like it's starting to affect your overall mood. And it's not just rain, of course. The bitter cold during the winter can take its toll, as can prolonged heat and humidity. So what's the deal? Can weather actually affect your mood, or is it just a figment of your imagination? In terms of the science behind whether the weather might affect your mood, well...it's debatable. The research is limited, and it varies.
